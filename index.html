Sequence Words

Sequence words help us show the order of events!

First
Next
Then
After that
Finally

First, I wake up in the morning.

Next, I brush my teeth.

Then, I eat breakfast.

After that, I get dressed.

Finally, I go to school.

Story Building with Linking Words

Let's build a story using different linking words!

Once upon a time, there was a little rabbit and his name was Hop.

Hop wanted to find a carrot but it started to rain.

He was sad because he was getting wet.

Then, he saw a big umbrella leaf.

He ran under the leaf so he could stay dry.

While he was under the leaf, he found a big orange carrot!

Finally, Hop was happy with his snack.

Complete the Story!
